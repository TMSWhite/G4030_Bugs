User's-Guide

User

How do I Answer?

Patients Patients are only allowed to use the Patient View.
With it, you can find out:
     Who is my Doctor?
     Where is my room?
     What infections do I have?
Clinicians Where are my patients?
     Use Patient wiew to search by MRN for a single patient
     Use MD view for all of your patients, sorted by month
     Use Location view, selecting your name and the desired month
What infections do they have?
     Use Patient or MD views - both contain sensitivity profiles
How severe is the infection?
     Use Location view - color coding shows level of resistance
What drug do they need?
     Use Patient or MD view, and scan down sensitivity profile for drugs in green
Nurses What is the acuity level of patients on my floor?
     Use Location view, selecting month - color coding shows level of resistance: purple > orange > yellow
Which patients require isolation?
     Use Location view - beds in red or orange require isolation.
          [Patients with the same acuity level, bugs, and sensitivity profile can share a room]
Housekeeping  Which rooms need special cleaning?
     Use Location view, selecting current month - rooms in purple and orange require special cleaning
Epidemiologists
Administators		  Which bugs are most common?
     Use Temporal view to see trends over time
     Use Location view, selecting by bug - will see total count
Which bugs are most resistant?
     Use Temporal view to see level of resistance over time
     Use Location view, selecting by bug and/or drug - will see total count
